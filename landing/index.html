Why Varna?

Homeless services have been around for a long time, but because of the skills gap in the nonprofit workforce and the technology industry, user friendly solutions have unfortunately not evolved to support vulnerable populations. Monetary, organizational resources and talent constraints have long fettered homeless services from providing a level of service present in many experiences.

The founders have already taken on the challenge of developing best in class tools to track shelter occupancy and electronic health record management for vulnerable populations; however, with the onset of COVID-19, this technology can be reutilized and paired with AI/IOT in emergency shelter situations to be a powerful solution in the ongoing crisis.

What Varna Provides?

Varna is an AI-powered analytics-based platform which acts as a one-stop destination for anyone looking for shelter and anyone who is managing a shelter.

  • Onboarding

    Varna makes it easy for shelters to start gathering information in a way that limits the risk to their staff and minimizes contact. There is a web-based app that any smartphone owner can use to start entering preliminary information en route to shelter sites.

  • Screening

    We use the best-in-class screening questions applied by hospitals delivered via mobile phone and in order to quickly identify symptomatic patients and direct them to be separated, triaged by onsite nurses, and placed in individual rooms if needed.

  • Monitoring

    Varna uses cutting edge AI cameras to precisely scan temperatures of individuals entering the shelter facility and can immediately flag if someone's temperature is above normal

Frequently Asked Questions

How to use Varna?
  • STEP 1 Once the seeker has the referral number, VarnaBot will do the pre-screening while they are en-route to the shelter.
  • STEP 2. At the shelter, show the QR code generated by VarnaBot to the camera.
  • STEP 3. Your temperature readings will be matched to your EHR.
  • STEP 4. You can get your allocation details by submitting your cellphone number in our site.
  • STEP 5. You’ll be asked to pick up a safe wearable to monitor your health status.
  • STEP 6. Shelter staff will assist you if you need any emergency medical care.

I have a referral number to a shelter. How do I sign up with Varna?

Talk to our chatbot- Varnabot. You’ll need to provide your cellphone number, answer some questions and we will assist you while you are en-route to your referred shelter.

I run a shelter. How can Varna help my shelter?

Varna is a SaaS solution that charges a license fee per organization and seat, combined with hardware costs associated with individualized IoT devices. Varna provides an AI-powered analytics-based platform for shelter managers to pre-screen, onboard, screen and monitor its occupants.

What technologies does Varna use?

Varna uses Web, Intelligence, Machine Learning and cutting edge IoT to run its analytics-based platform.

How can Varna help me during a pandemic to get shelter?

Varna would screen you for any existing symptoms, makes your shelter onboarding smooth, screens you, allocates a bed, monitors your symptoms and connects you to expert medical care during a pandemic.

What does a shelter provide me?

A shelter would provide me free bed, meals, assistance and emergency medical care.

What area do you serve?

We primarily serve the homeless and medically vulnerable population of Los Angeles and Orange County.
